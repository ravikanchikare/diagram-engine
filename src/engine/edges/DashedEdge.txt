DashedEdge

Primary shape

source - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -> target

Stepped example

source - - - - - +
                  |
                  + - - - - - - - -> target

Visual characteristics

- Smooth-step edge with rounded bends.
- Dashed stroke by default (`6 6`) unless the edge style overrides it.
- Rounded line caps so dash ends stay soft.
- Arrowhead comes from the supplied `markerEnd`.
