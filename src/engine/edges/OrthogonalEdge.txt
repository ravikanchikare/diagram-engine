OrthogonalEdge

Primary shape

source -------+
              |
              +-------> target

Top-entry variant

source -------+
              |
              v
            target

Visual characteristics

- Horizontal and vertical segments only.
- Corners are rounded instead of hard 90-degree joints.
- Default routing moves horizontally first, then vertically.
- If the target enters from the top, the final segment drops vertically into it.
- Arrowhead comes from the supplied `markerEnd`.
